What We Offer

Our Professional Services

From everyday home cleaning to specialized commercial solutions, we have you covered.

Residential Cleaning

Regular Home Cleaning

Weekly or bi-weekly cleaning to keep your home spotless. Dusting, vacuuming, mopping, kitchen and bathroom sanitation.

Deep Cleaning

Thorough top-to-bottom cleaning including behind appliances, inside cabinets, grout scrubbing, and sanitization.

Move-In / Move-Out

Complete cleaning for tenants moving in or out. We ensure the property is pristine and ready for new occupants.

Post-Construction

Removal of dust, debris, and residues left after renovations or construction projects. We make it livable again.

Commercial Cleaning

Offices & Workspaces

Daily or scheduled cleaning for offices of all sizes. Desks, common areas, restrooms, and breakrooms kept immaculate.

Retail & Storefronts

Keep your business inviting for customers. Floor care, display cleaning, window washing, and restroom maintenance.

Common Areas & Buildings

Lobbies, hallways, stairwells, and shared facilities. We keep residential and commercial buildings welcoming.

Specialty Services

Window Cleaning

Crystal-clear windows inside and out. We handle any height and type, leaving streak-free, brilliant results.

Upholstery & Carpets

Professional steam cleaning and stain removal for sofas, chairs, rugs, and carpets. Fresh and allergen-free.

Floor Treatment

Stripping, waxing, polishing, and sealing for all floor types. Hardwood, tile, marble, and concrete floors.

Landscaping & Exteriors

Garden maintenance, lawn care, hedge trimming, pressure washing of driveways, patios, and building facades.
